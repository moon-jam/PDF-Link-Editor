    PDF Link Editor

    Fix broken links and add new clickable links, entirely in your browser.

    How to use

    1. Open a PDF and scroll to browse the pages.
    2. Click Add link, then drag a box over the area you want clickable.
    3. Choose where it points: top of a page, a clicked spot, the current view, or a URL.
    4. Fix links already in the file from the right panel: re-point, resize, or delete.
    5. Click Export to download the new PDF. The original look is untouched.

    Good to know

    • Single-click a link to edit it, double-click to follow it.
    • Hover a selected link, then drag its edge to move or a corner to resize.
    • ⌘Z / ⌘⇧Z to undo and redo.
